I‚Äôm Thabata Marchi, a Mobile Developer and Digital Accessibility Specialist. As the founder of the **Web Sem Barreiras** community in Brazil, my purpose is to connect people and transform lives by creating truly accessible digital products, promoting real and practical inclusion.

I work with React Native, Flutter, React.js, and lead initiatives to broaden the conversation around digital accessibility. I am recognized in the field and have delivered courses, lectures, and consulting sessions on inclusive technology.
